Star Web Co. Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Star Web Co. collects through this website, why it is collected, and the choices available to visitors, prospects, clients, and giveaway entrants.

Last updated: July 15, 2026.

1. Who We Are

Star Web Co. is a Virginia web-design business operated by Brycen Pekrul. Privacy questions or requests may be sent to hello@starwebco.com.

2. Information You Provide

Depending on how you use the website, Star Web Co. may collect information you voluntarily submit, including your name, business name, role, email address, telephone number, website URL, industry, location or service area, project goals, requested services, budget, timeline, messages, support requests, documents, invoices, and payment-related status information.

Do not submit passwords, Social Security numbers, bank-account information, medical information, or other highly sensitive information through public website forms.

3. Giveaway Information

When you enter the Star Web Co. Website Giveaway, Star Web Co. may collect your name, business name, role, email, phone number, business website or social page, industry, Virginia location or service area, website goals, entry timestamp, time zone, eligibility confirmation, rules acceptance, and optional marketing consent.

This information is used to administer the giveaway, verify eligibility, identify duplicate or fraudulent entries, determine the first 10 eligible entries, select and contact a potential winner, deliver the prize and discounts, maintain promotion records, and comply with legal obligations.

Marketing consent is optional. Selecting the optional checkbox allows Star Web Co. to contact you about website services, business tips, and entrant-only promotional offers. Declining marketing consent does not affect eligibility or odds.

4. Quote and Contact Forms

Quote and giveaway forms are processed through Formspree. Form submissions may be stored by Formspree and delivered to Star Web Co. by email or through a Formspree dashboard. Formspree’s handling of information is governed by its own terms and privacy practices.

5. Client Portal

The Star Web Co. client portal uses Firebase services for authentication and project data. Client information may include project details, document status, invoices, payment status, recurring-service status, and support requests. Access is restricted through account authentication and applicable Firebase security rules.

6. Analytics and Technical Information

The website uses Google Analytics to understand general website usage. Analytics data may include device type, browser, approximate location, referral source, pages viewed, and interactions. This information is used to improve website performance, content, marketing, and usability.

Web servers, hosting providers, security services, and analytics providers may automatically process IP addresses, timestamps, user-agent data, and diagnostic logs needed to deliver and secure the website.

7. How Information Is Used

  • Responding to inquiries and providing requested quotes or services;
  • Managing website projects, contracts, invoices, support, and maintenance;
  • Administering the giveaway and related entrant benefits;
  • Communicating about services when permitted;
  • Improving website design, performance, security, and marketing;
  • Preventing spam, fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access; and
  • Complying with legal, tax, accounting, and recordkeeping obligations.

8. Sharing of Information

Star Web Co. does not sell personal information to unrelated third parties. Information may be shared with service providers that help operate the website and business, such as Formspree, Google, Firebase, Cloudflare, GitHub, payment processors, email providers, and professional advisers, only as reasonably needed for their services.

Information may also be disclosed when reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful process, enforce agreements, investigate fraud or security incidents, or protect rights and safety.

9. Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, including project administration, promotion records, business operations, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution. Giveaway entry records may be retained after the drawing to document administration and eligibility.

10. Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of information by emailing hello@starwebco.com. Some information may need to be retained for active contracts, billing, security, promotion administration, or legal obligations.

You may opt out of promotional email using the unsubscribe method provided in the message or by contacting Star Web Co. Administrative giveaway notices, project communications, invoices, and support messages may still be sent when necessary.

11. Security

Star Web Co. uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards appropriate to a small web-design business. No internet transmission, email system, or online storage method is guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. Children

This website and the giveaway are not directed to children under 13. The giveaway is limited to entrants age 18 or older. Star Web Co. does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the giveaway.

13. External Links

The website may link to third-party websites, platforms, or portfolio demonstrations. Star Web Co. is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party services.

14. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated as services, technology, or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of the page will be revised when material updates are published.

15. Contact

Questions or privacy requests may be sent to hello@starwebco.com.
