# Cold Call Script

Hi, is this [Business Name]?

My name is Brycen with Star Web Co. I help local businesses build modern websites that look professional and make it easier for customers to contact them.

I was looking at local [industry] businesses and noticed your online presence could be improved. I wanted to see if you were open to a quick conversation about updating your website or improving how customers find you online.

If interested:
Great. I can take a quick look at your current website and send over a few ideas. What's the best email to send that to?

If not interested:
No worries at all. Thanks for your time.
